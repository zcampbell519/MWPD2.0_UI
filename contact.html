Good for You, Good for Montana

Buying products from Montana's forests has a powerful effect. It strengthens and sustains things that matter, including our forests. In our state, the wood product industry also benefits:

  • Montana's Economy
  • Recreation
  • Tourism
  • Water Quality
  • Carbon Storage
  • Wildfire Reduction
  • Hunting and Fishing
  • Rural Communities
